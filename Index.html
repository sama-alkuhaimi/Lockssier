About Us

Our Story

Hi! I'm a 12-year-old who loves lip balms and everything about lip care. I've always noticed many girls love buying popular beauty products from big stores like Sephora because they look cute and trendy. But most people don't really check what's inside them or understand the long lists of ingredients they contain.

I wanted something that would keep my lips soft and healthy, so I started researching and found the perfect recipe with natural ingredients. After lots of learning (and a few fun tries!), I made my own lip balm.

That's how my little business began. Now, I get to share my lip balms with others who love taking care of their lips too. My goal is to inspire other kids (and adults!) to believe in themselves and know that age doesn't matter — you can do anything if you're passionate and determined!

💚

Our Mission

To create pure, natural lip care products that make you feel beautiful — while proving that passion and determination can take you anywhere.

FAQ

Are your products 100% natural?
Our Organic Lip Balm is made with 100% all-natural ingredients. Our glosses contain carefully selected ingredients for the perfect finish.
When will the Honey Lip Scrub be available?
Our Honey Lip Scrub is launching soon! Follow us on Instagram @_.Lockssier for the official release date.
Why did you choose the name "Lockssier"?
Glossier has really inspired us to name our brand after them! "Lock" doesn't really represent any brand, but we thought it would be nice to show our lips locked in with our lip care. While "Ssier" stands for Glossier. So Lockssier!
